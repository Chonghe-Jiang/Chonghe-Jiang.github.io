Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained a bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics (2019-2023; Elite Class) from Tongji University supervised by Prof. Zhaolin Hu .

My current research interests mainly focus on optimization problems that arise from machine learning, data science and signal processing, especially in nonsmooth optimization and distributed optimization(computing). I am also interested in applied probability and stochastic simulation theory.
