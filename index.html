Biography

I am a fourth-year Ph.D student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Shiqian Ma and Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So . Before joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Statistics from Nankai University. I mainly focus on convex/nonconvex constrained optimization problems that arise from machine learning, statistics, and signal processing.
