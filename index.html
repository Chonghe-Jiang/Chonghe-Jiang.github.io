Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained a bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics (2019-2023; Elite Class) from Tongji University supervised by Prof. Zhaolin Hu .

My current research interests lie in continuous optimization and its application to machine learning and operation research. I am also interested in stochastic simulation and causal inference.
