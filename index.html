Biography

I am a second-year M.Phil student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained a bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics (2019-2023; Elite Class) from Tongji University supervised by Prof. Zhaolin Hu .

My current research interests lie in continuous optimization theory (e.g. SDP tightness, local regularity for constrained optimization) and its application to machine learning, operation research, and economics. Previously, I also did research related to simulation theory (simulation optimization) and stochastic programming.
